Kovalam: A renowned beach destination in Kerala, famous for its crescent-shaped coastline, golden sands, and turquoise waters, offering a perfect blend of relaxation, water sports, and stunning sunsets along the Arabian Sea.

Kumarakom

⭐ 3.6
Kumarakom: A tranquil backwater village in Kerala, nestled along the Vembanad Lake, renowned for its serene beauty, lush landscapes, and rich biodiversity, offering a peaceful escape amidst picturesque surroundings and traditional houseboat experiences.

Allepy

⭐ 4.7
Known as the "Venice of the East," this coastal town in Kerala is famed for its intricate network of backwaters, tranquil canals, and vibrant houseboat cruises, offering a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and leisurely experiences.
