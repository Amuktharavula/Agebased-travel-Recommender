Swarnagiri Temple

⭐ 4.5
The Swarnagiri Venkateswara Swamy Temple, also known as Yadadri Tirumala Devasthanam, is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara in Bhuvanagiri district

Ramappa Temple

⭐ 4.6
Ramappa Temple, also known as the Rudreshwara temple, is a Kakatiya style Hindu temple dedicated to the Hindu god Shiva, located in Palampet village, Mulugu district, Telangana

BHADRACHALAM

⭐ 4.4
Bhadrachalam, located in Telangana, is a renowned pilgrimage town along the banks of the Godavari River, famous for its ancient temple dedicated to Lord Rama and revered as a sacred site by devotees for its spiritual significance and scenic beauty.
