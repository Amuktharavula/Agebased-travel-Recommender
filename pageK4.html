Wayanad

⭐ 4.5
Wayanad: Nestled amidst the Western Ghats in Kerala, Wayanad is a picturesque hill station renowned for its lush greenery, misty mountains, wildlife sanctuaries, and serene ambiance, offering a perfect retreat for nature lovers and adventurers alike.

Houseboat Cruise

⭐ 4.1
Kerala Houseboat Cruise: A mesmerizing journey through the tranquil backwaters of Kerala aboard traditional houseboats, offering a unique experience of floating along palm-fringed canals, lush paddy fields, and quaint villages, immersing oneself in the serene beauty and rich culture of God's Own Country.

Kochi

⭐ 4.4
