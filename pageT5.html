Kumbakonam

⭐ 4.3
Kumbakonam: A historic town in Tamil Nadu, renowned for its intricately carved temples, vibrant culture, and traditional craftsmanship, preserving the essence of South Indian heritage and spirituality.

Kanyakumari

⭐ 4.4
Kanyakumari: The southernmost tip of mainland India, where the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean converge, renowned for its stunning sunrises, sunsets, and the iconic Vivekananda Rock Memorial, offering a breathtaking fusion of natural beauty and spiritual significance.

Mahabalipuram

⭐ 4.4
Mahabalipuram: A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Tamil Nadu, famed for its ancient rock-cut temples, intricately carved sculptures, and stunning shore temple, showcasing the rich architectural legacy of the Pallava dynasty against the backdrop of the Bay of Bengal.
